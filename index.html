The Thinking

This is where I start. Proactive work — ideas made without a brief, or well beyond what the brief asked for. The work that follows is the proof. This is the reason.

01 Campaign Idea · OOH

Foodpanda covered its own billboards for Ramadan. And won.

During fasting hours, Foodpanda's outdoor ads drew curtains over themselves. The logo became "foodpaRda." An act of cultural empathy that turned restraint into the most-talked-about OOH of the month — and asked nothing from the audience except respect.

↗
02 Campaign Idea · Experiential

Corona gave away free concert tickets. The catch: clean the beach first.

The stage was curtained. The music only started when a target weight of beach plastic had been collected. A weigh-scale drove the reveal. Metallica, Coldplay, Beyoncé — all behind a curtain that the crowd moved together to lift. Sustainability as spectacle, not sermon.

↗
03 Tech Idea · Product

Google Maps as a fog radar for highway pile-ups

4G connectivity, phone-to-phone signals, no new hardware. A Maps feature that tells you what's stopped ahead — even when you can't see it. A human problem solved with existing technology.

↗
04 Tech Idea · Experiential

Google's Silent Visarjan — a festival, without the noise ban

Mumbai's Ganesh festival faced noise restrictions. Google's answer: Bluetooth speakers, A-list DJs, and 10,000 people dancing silently through the streets. Technology as cultural bridge, not cultural replacement.

↗
05 Spec · Brand Strategy

What Is Sleep? — A philosophical campaign for Hästens

Not a product ad. An inquiry. Into rest, recovery, and what we lose when we accept less than extraordinary sleep. Built without a brief because the brand deserved better than what briefs usually produce.

About

"I'm not a copywriter who occasionally thinks about strategy. I'm a creative thinker who happens to be lethal with language."

Over ten years across Mumbai agency floors, global in-house teams, and a one-person studio in Malmö — the range is deliberate. Agency discipline. Client-side accountability. Independent freedom. Each one made the thinking better.

I've written for audiences from UHNW whisky collectors to first-time digital banking users, in categories from luxury goods to fast food to financial services. The method doesn't change. Start with a true human insight. Find the surprising angle. Execute with craft.

I work at the intersection of strategy, language, and cultural fluency. I understand AI creative tools not as someone who fears them, but as someone who has built with them and knows precisely where the human has to step back in. That gap is where I work.

Based in Malmö. Open to Europe and beyond. Full-time, freelance, or consulting — for the right brief.
