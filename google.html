01

Make Google Do It

Google Assistant was new to Indian consumers. The brief was to show what it could actually do — in a way that felt like an everyday Indian life, not a product demo. Four films, four relatable moments, one simple idea: you don't need to do it yourself anymore.

02

Money Answers Made Easy

Google Search as a financial advisor — accessible, judgment-free, always available. The insight: Indians ask their phones the financial questions they're embarrassed to ask anyone else. The film made that relatable without making it patronising.

Campaign Film

03

Google My Business

Small business owners in India were the audience — people running shops, restaurants, and services who had never thought of themselves as having an "online presence." The film made Google My Business feel like the most natural thing in the world.

"The best brief for a tech product is always a human brief."

Three campaigns, three different Google products, three different human insights. The common thread across all of them: technology is only interesting when it solves a problem someone actually has. Start there and the campaign writes itself.
