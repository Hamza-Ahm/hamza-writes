Make Google Do It — Launch of Google Assistant in India

The launch campaign for Google Assistant in India. The insight: life is full of more interesting and important things to do. So let your assistant take care of the rest. Four films, four everyday Indian moments, one simple truth — you don't have to do everything yourself anymore.

02

Money Answers Made Easy

Google Search as a financial advisor — accessible, judgment-free, always available. Indians ask their phones the financial questions they're too embarrassed to ask anyone else. The film made that relatable without making it patronising.

03

Google My Business

Small business owners in India who had never thought of themselves as having an "online presence." The film made Google My Business feel like the most natural next step for their business — not a technical barrier.
