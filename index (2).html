01Campaign Idea · OOH

Foodpanda covered its own billboards for Ramadan. And won.

During fasting hours, Foodpanda's outdoor ads drew curtains over themselves. The logo became "foodpaRda." An act of cultural empathy that turned restraint into the most-talked-about OOH of the month.

About

"I'm not a copywriter who occasionally thinks about strategy. I'm a creative thinker who happens to be lethal with language."

Over ten years across Mumbai agency floors, global in-house teams, and a one-person studio in Malmö — the range is deliberate. Agency discipline. Client-side accountability. Independent freedom.

I've written for audiences from UHNW whisky collectors to first-time digital banking users, across luxury goods, fast food, finance, tech, and beauty. The method doesn't change. Start with a true human insight. Find the surprising angle. Execute with craft.

I work at the intersection of strategy, language, and cultural fluency. Based in Malmö. Open to Europe and beyond.
