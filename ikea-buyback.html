You Bring It Back, Every Day.

Brand
IKEA
Type

Global · 31 Markets
Deliverables
Print Campaign

The IKEA Buyback & Resell Service lets customers sell their used IKEA furniture back to the store. The brief was to communicate this year-round — not just as a promotion, but as a fundamental part of how IKEA thinks about furniture and the planet.

The campaign ran across 31 markets. The idea: show the furniture in the middle of a real life transition. On a tram moving to a new city. On a boat setting sail for a new home. On an escalator going back to shine again.

The copy did the heavy lifting — "You bring it back, we buy it back, every day." "Setting sail for a new home." "It's time to shine again." Each line earned its place alongside the image.

"31 markets. One idea. Furniture that never really leaves."

A sustainability message that didn't feel like a sustainability message. It felt like an invitation to be part of something — a circular system that benefits everyone. The furniture doesn't end. It just finds a new story.
