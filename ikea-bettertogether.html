We're Better Together.

Brand
IKEA
Type
Brand Film
Role
Copywriter · Creative
Category
Home · Belonging

A brand film for IKEA that goes beyond the product entirely. Home isn't a place. It's the people in it. And the things we share make it what it is.

The brief was to communicate IKEA's belief that home is better when shared. Not a campaign about furniture. A campaign about what furniture enables — the shared meals, the compromises, the arguments about where to put the sofa, the life that happens around the things you own together.

The film found warmth without reaching for it. That's the hardest thing to do in home retail — and the most important.

"Home isn't a place. It's the people in it."

The film worked because it didn't try to sell anything. It just showed something true about the way people live together — and let IKEA stand quietly behind that truth. Restraint as a creative choice. The brand earning its place in the moment rather than demanding it.
